Visit Los Angeles!

Enjoy this busy yet grounded city jam packed full of historic sites and entertainment opportunities. From the downtown stride to the hills of glamour, there's something for everyone!

Top 3 things to do in LA

Walk of Fame

Walk along the stars in Hollywood blvd and keep an eye out for a famous figure you love!

Griffith Observatory

Take a tour around the famous observatory with a great view of Los Angeles and the sunset/stars!

Venice Beach

Keep cool and fresh when you visit the exciting beach town full of restaruants and entertainment on every corner of the pier and boardwalk!
